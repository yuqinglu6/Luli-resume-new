  • Traveling

    • Every year on my birthday, I make it a point to visit a new place, explore unseen landscapes, and try activities I haven't done before, keeping my appreciation for the world's diversity alive. Whenever I have the chance during important holidays or anniversaries, I also embark on trips to experience a different environment, relishing moments that break away from my daily routine. These adventures serve as wonderful refreshers in my life.

  • Listening to music

    • During my school years, my favorite artist was JJ Lin. His songs provided me with strength and resonated deeply with me at that time. I don’t have a specific favorite genre; I choose songs based on how I feel, often switching it up according to my mood. I love going to live music venues to celebrate with others, and I also enjoy cranking up the volume at home to immerse myself in my own world. There's something special about sharing earbuds with friends in a crowd, listening to a song that belongs just to us. Music is magical—gentle yet powerful, mysterious yet authentic; it’s a beautiful expression of the surreal and romantic.
